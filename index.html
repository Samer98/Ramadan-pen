Ramadan Pen — How to use

  • Draw: Move your finger in front of the camera. Hold the screen (touch or click and hold) to pause drawing; release to draw again.
  • Clear & cancel: Press Space on desktop, or tap the Clear button on mobile.
  • Recording: Recording starts when you draw and stops automatically after a short idle. Each recording gets a unique link you can share.
  • Share: After a recording is saved, use Share, Copy link, or Download. Anyone who opens the link can watch the video.

Make sure the camera can see your hand.
